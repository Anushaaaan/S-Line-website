IR Thermometer UT300S

UT300 series is an economical infrared thermometer. Its compact and simple design makes it easier for users to carry and operate. Designed with high accuracy, high repeatability, quick response, long measuring distance, and more features, UT300A+ is suitable for measuring the surface temperature of hard-to-reach or dangerous objects.
