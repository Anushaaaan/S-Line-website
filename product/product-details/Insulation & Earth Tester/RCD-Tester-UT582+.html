RCD Tester UT582+

UT582+ is mainly used to detect whether the RCD in the circuit can be triggered normally. It is only suitable for single-phase circuits with the supply voltage range of 195V~253V. Its voltage measurement range is 30V~600V, 45Hz ~65Hz.
