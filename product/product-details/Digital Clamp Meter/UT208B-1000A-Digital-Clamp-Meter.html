UT200 Series 1000A True RMS Digital Clamp Meters

UT200 series are 6000-count digital clamp meters with auto range, large LCD, true RMS, autoscale memory, and NCV functions. They conform to CAT III 1000V/CAT IV 600V safety rating andcertiﬁed by CE/ETL. The UT206B, UT207B, and UT208B come with LoZ mode for eliminating ‘ghost ’voltage, LPF ACV function for measuring INV and VFD voltage, and inrush current modefor capturing transient current. The UT206B and UT208B also feature high-precision temperature measurement with 0.1 °C resolution, and can be equipped with a ﬂexible current probe toextend the AC current measurement range to 3000A.
Models in UT200 series: UT205E, UT206B, UT207B, UT208B
