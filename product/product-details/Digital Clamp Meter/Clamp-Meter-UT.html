Clamp Meter UT 219E

UT219 series is 600A true RMS professional clamp meter with CAT IV 600V rating. It is industrially designed with IP54 rating and able to withstand 2 meters drop test for measurement in harsh environments. This series is suitable for professional electricians, HVAC/R and motor service technicians.
Models in UT219 series: UT219E, UT219M, UT219DS
