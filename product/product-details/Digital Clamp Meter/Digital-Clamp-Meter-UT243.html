UT243 Power and Harmonics Clamp Meter

UT243 is an intelligent, handheld and stable power and harmonics clamp meter, with functions of digital ammeter and power meter. It can measure voltage, current, active/reactive power, power factor, phase angle and more. UT243 series can measure single harmonic components up to 20 times.
