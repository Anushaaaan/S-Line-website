Digital Multimeter UT195M

UT195 series are handheld industrial digital multimeters with IP65 and 2m drop proof. Compare with traditional multimeters, UT195’s troubleshooting capability, resolution, and precision are improved to find issues related to motor drive, factory equipment maintenance, electrical distribution, and repair etc. It is exquisitely designed and easy to operate.
