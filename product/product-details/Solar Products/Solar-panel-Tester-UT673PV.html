Solar panel Tester UT673PV

UT673PV solar MPPT meter can effectively identify any abnormalities in solar panels by testing their maximum power, peak power voltage, peak power current, open circuit voltage, and short circuit current. Featuring a spacious screen and automatic measurement capabilities, this device conveniently displays all measurement results simultaneously. Its portable design enables easy carrying, greatly enhancing the operational and maintenance efficiency for photovoltaic manufacturers, installers, and end users.
