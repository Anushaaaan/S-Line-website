LCR Meter UT612

UT612 LCR meter is used to measure inductance, capacitance and resistance. Advanced features include series/parallel measurement modes, quality factor/loss/phase angle measurements, equivalent/DC resistance measurements and 5 selectable frequencies in AC mode. UT612 can be used in production lines, component inspection and electronic maintenance.
