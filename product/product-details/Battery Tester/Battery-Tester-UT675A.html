Battery Tester - UT675A

UT673A/UT675A battery testers can be used to analyze the battery health of automobile batteries, suitable for battery capacities from 3Ah to 250Ah. These CE certified testers can rapidly and accurately detect common faults caused by battery. UT675A also includes USB communication and built-in printer for report generation.
